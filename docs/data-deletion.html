Data Deletion Request

Family Expense Tracker by TrueCube

Family Expense Tracker allows you to delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app. No need to contact us — you have full control.

How to Delete Your Data

Steps to delete your account and data:

  1. Open Family Expense Tracker on your device
  2. Tap the Family tab in the bottom navigation bar
  3. Scroll down to the Account & Privacy section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Read the warning and tap Continue
  6. Enter your password to confirm, then tap Delete My Account

Your account and data will be permanently deleted immediately.

What Data Is Deleted

Data Type What Happens
User profile (email, display name) Permanently deleted
Authentication credentials (password) Permanently deleted
Family group membership Removed immediately
Expense records you created Permanently deleted if you are the sole family member;
Retained for remaining members if others are in the group
Income records you created Permanently deleted if you are the sole family member;
Retained for remaining members if others are in the group
Investment records you created Permanently deleted if you are the sole family member;
Retained for remaining members if others are in the group
Custom categories (expense, income, investment) Deleted with family group if you are the sole member;
Retained for remaining members if others are in the group
Anonymous usage analytics Retained in aggregate form (not linked to your identity)

Retention Details

  • If you are the only member of your family group: all shared data (expenses, income, investments, categories, and the family group itself) is permanently deleted along with your account. No data is retained.
  • If other members remain in your family group: your user profile and authentication are deleted, and you are removed from the family. The shared financial records remain accessible to the remaining family members since they contributed to and rely on that shared data.
  • Deletion is immediate. There is no grace period or recovery option. Once confirmed, the action cannot be undone.
  • Anonymous analytics data collected by Firebase Analytics is retained in aggregate form. This data is not linked to your identity and cannot be used to identify you.

Can't Access the App?

If you are unable to access the app to delete your account (for example, if you no longer have the app installed), you can request account deletion by emailing us:

Email: support@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your account. We will verify your identity and process the deletion within 7 business days.
