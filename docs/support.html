Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a family group?

After signing up and logging in, you'll be prompted to create a new family or join an existing one. Choose "Create Family", enter a name, and you'll receive a 6-character invite code to share with family members.

How do I invite family members?

Go to the Family tab in the app. Your invite code is displayed there — share it with your spouse, partner, or family members. They can enter the code when they sign up to join your family group.

How do I customize categories?

Go to the Family tab and scroll to "Manage Categories". You can add or remove expense categories, income sources, and investment types to match your family's needs.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link from Firebase.

How do I delete my account?

Go to the Family tab, scroll to "Account & Privacy", and tap "Delete Account". You'll be asked to confirm and re-enter your password. All your data will be permanently deleted.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. All data is stored on Google Firebase with enterprise-grade encryption in transit and at rest. We never have access to your password, and your data is only accessible to members of your family group.
